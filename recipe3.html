Tomato soup

Ingredients

  • Two 28 ounce cans San Marzano tomatoes
  • 1 stick(8 tablespons) salted butter
  • 1 yellow onion, peel removed and cut into 4 chuncks
  • 3-4 cloves smashed garlic(optional)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of salt

Instructions

  1. Put the tomatoes, butter, and onion in a large saucepan or Dutch oven. Bring it up to a low bubble, then turn the heat to medium low. Cover partially with a lid; let it simmer for 45 minutes. Stir every 15 minutes or so to prevent scorching on the bottom.
  2. Remove the onion pieces using tongs.You can leave the garlic if you want.
  3. Using the immersion blender, blend up the soup until it's smooth as you want it. Taste and adjust;you can thin it out with milk,water,broth,cream, whatever you want;I just leave it as-is.
  4. Serve tomato soup with garlic bread, grilled cheese, croutons, crackers, pestos, whatever you like.
