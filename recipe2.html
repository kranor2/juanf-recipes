Green salad recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vineger
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. shake dressing ingredients up in a jar.Taste and adjust to get it just right for how you like it
  2. Toss a gnerous amount of spring greens with just a few light drizzles of dressing- you want to get the leaves entirely coated in dressing(but not so much that they get soggy) a gentle toss prevents breaking the leaves./li>
